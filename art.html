ART

When I was in High School, I was struggling with depression and suicidal thoughts. My underdeveloped, teenaged brain did not know how to handle certain things emotionally, and my solution was self-harm.

During a text message conversation with a friend, I said that I am a "Suicidal Misery Machine", meaning that I exist for the sole purpose of being miserable and making others miserable.

From that conversation came my band name, and I credited my music and art to Suicidal Death Machine. Though my music was low quality, and I wasn't particularly good at playing instruments, I had fun doing it. A majority of the fun probably came from the art that I made as a result. Almost everything includes my signature cog with blood spatter.
