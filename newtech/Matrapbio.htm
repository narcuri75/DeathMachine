Nathan Arcuri was born under the Pacific Ocean in a bio-dome on June 26, 20XX. While living under the sea he would calculate the water pressure so that if there was too much pressure they could do something about it rather than being crushed by trillions and trillions of tons of salt water and suffering an excruciatingly painful death. Nathan Arcuri was drafted into the Intergalactic War of 25XX on the planet of Nebulon-5. He lost his left leg while undergoing the satchel deployment on the planets main control center. The satchel exploded too soon and scraps of intergalactic particle board, from the newly refurbished office building, were then dislodged in his leg rendering it completely useless. He is luckier than his 12 partners, they were tossed through the air like rag dolls. Some, he says, were ejected from the 459 story tower and upon hitting the ground, did not die instantly. All of his partners were about 35 feet away from the satchel whereas Nathan was only 3 feet away from the satchel when it detonated. He is still confused at the fact that he is still alive. He used to try to get over his lost leg and make up rhymes about addition and subtraction; he found out that he was quite good. Not at math but at rhyming. He then went on to win 13 platinum records with his hit songs, thus making enough money to afford a 600 billion dollar robotic prosthetic leg which was connected directly to his nerves and tendons making it almost as if he never lost his leg in the first place. He now lives normally in his 10 trillion dollar home in Sacramento, CA. Recent news states that he was planning on taking a time machine back to the year 2007 to promote his new hit song “A+ in Math” to get people to understand math properly so we don’t end up with another C average president, which he says “There have been Several.”
