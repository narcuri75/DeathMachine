About This Page

This section was meant to show evidence of my ability to collaborate with others.

Since it was a project-based school, most of the work I did was in groups. I’m not sure why I didn’t include more examples here, but I did include Law Day again, along with a page I made for the yearbook.

The odd thing was that you had to request to be in yearbook and be approved. I never actually wanted to be in the yearbook class. In fact, I felt like an outcast there in a lot of ways. I made a page that I thought was great, was told to change it several times, and eventually it became...whatever is shown here. Which I am not exactly proud of.

Then they changed it again without telling me before the final print. Which makes sense, I guess. It isn't the best looking page ever, but how was that supposed to make me feel?
