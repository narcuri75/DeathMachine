About This Page

More Flash! This one is a lot less straightforward than the guitar buttons, so I added buttons below to make navigation easier. The original idea was that the user would click the knobs on the amplifier to navigate the “Learning Outcomes.”

The Learning Outcomes section was where I displayed the projects I worked on in high school, organized under 10 different Learning Outcome categories. A few projects appear in more than one category, but that was at the request of my teachers, mostly to “fill it out” more, since I tended to make creative projects more than anything else.
