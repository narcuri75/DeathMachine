About This Page

Whoa! You found my super secret page!

Like many other projects, I managed to have some downtime and decided to add a bunch of extra stuff to my portfolio.

To house all of this, I needed another over-the-top Flash animation GUI, so I made the NRA GEAR, which is a play on my initials, not the stupid gun association. I got in trouble one too many times for putting “gun stuff” on my school projects.

I added art, or at least one portrait of Stephen Colbert steam cleaning the American flag. I don’t really know why.

I added music that I had recorded that is since gone. I had the voice of an angel, once upon a time.

I also added games that I made in Flash. Well, “made” is generous. They were game templates, and I mostly just changed the sprites.
