About This Page

This section was meant to show evidence of my proficiency in thinking critically.

My school would sometimes merge classes together, so one of my examples is from my Algebra II/Chemistry class. The other is from my Political Science class, where we attended Law Day. We traveled to a courthouse in the Bay Area and performed mock trials in a real courtroom, with real attorneys and judges.

A friend of mine did none of the work for his role, he simply resorted to denying everything while on the stand and generally being uncooperative. He ended up being awarded an iPod touch.
