Today's Meals

Basics:

  • 🍗 Feed fish once every 12 hours.
  • 💊 Add drops of medication to the tank when they are ill.
  • 🧽 Scrub the tank with the sponge to clean it.
  • 🥄 Scoop fish into storage if needed.
  • 🐟 Move fish between tank and storage.
  • 🌿 Move decor between tank and storage.
  • 🎨 Customize your tank.
  • 🛒 Buy fish, decor, customizations, and equipment.
  • ❔ Review tips for fish comfort.
  • ⚙️ Change Settings, Export/Import save data, and Reset game data.

Tips:

  • 💔 Missing a meal removes half a heart.
  • 💖 4 meals in a row restore half a heart.
  • 💩 Fish poop 1 to 3 hours after eating and it settles on the gravel.
  • 🏚️ Bigger fish and more fish make grime build up faster.
  • 🐠 Sucker fish and better filters help slow grime build-up.

Hardware Acceleration

For a smooth gameplay experience, please make sure to enable hardware acceleration in your browser.

How to enable hardware acceleration:

Google Chrome & Brave
  1. Click the three-dot menu (upper right)
  2. Settings
  3. System
  4. Toggle on Use graphics acceleration when available.
Microsoft Edge
  1. Click the three-dot menu
  2. Settings
  3. System and performance
  4. Toggle on Use hardware acceleration when available.
Firefox
  1. Click the menu button (three horizontal lines)
  2. Settings
  3. General
  4. Scroll to Performance
  5. Uncheck "Use recommended performance settings"
  6. Check Use hardware acceleration when available.
